About

Tarrowby Holdings is a private holding company focused on the Mid-Atlantic mid-market. We acquire and hold businesses for the long term, typically in industrial and services sectors. Our portfolio companies operate independently with their own management teams; the corporate office focuses on capital allocation, governance, and strategic support.

The firm was originally founded in 1998 and has operated under William Tarrowby's leadership since 2012. We are not a private equity fund with fixed hold periods. We hold what we own.
