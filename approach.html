Investment Approach

We acquire majority or full positions in profitable, established businesses in industrial and services sectors. We target companies with $5M to $30M in EBITDA, with management teams that want to keep building rather than sell and leave.

We do not impose private-equity-style operating playbooks. Each portfolio company keeps its identity, its management, and its strategic plan. Our role is governance, capital allocation, and selective M&A support.

We hold for the long term. Several of our current portfolio companies have been part of Tarrowby for more than fifteen years. This horizon shapes everything we do: how we structure deals, how we work with management, how we measure success.
